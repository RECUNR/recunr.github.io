About Us

We are a group of students who aim to create an environment for like-minded individuals to explore and tinker with electronics. The club is structured around a central project and several side-projects, many of which, are to be presented at Maker Faire at L.A., Reno, and Las Vegas. This year's side projects include a weather baloon, plasma arc (speaker), and a few arduino and raspberry pi musical animation projects. The main project is an active RC car chase and seek implementation using computer vision.
